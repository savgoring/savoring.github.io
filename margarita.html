MARGARITA RECIPE
  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen limeade
  • 6 fluid ounces tequila
  • fluid ounces triple sec
  • 1 cup ice

In a blender, add in the crushed ice. Pour in limeade concentrate, tequila and triple sec. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.

Please refer to our Safe Serve page for appropriate alcohol intake
