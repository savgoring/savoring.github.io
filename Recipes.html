BELLINI RECIPE
  • 1 oz Peach Shnapps
  • 3 oz dry Champagne
  • 2 oz peach nectar
  • 1/2 cup crushed ice

Mix the peach nectar and schnapps in a chilled glass. Add half a cup of crushed ice, stir, and add the champagne. May be prepared in a blender to serve frozen drink. (Serves 2)

Please refer to our Safe Serve page for appropriate alcohol intake
